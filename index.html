The Compiler's note, on the final leaf

Here my hand stops. I have given you everything the Archive holds — the road, the rain, the man riding into both.

The pages that remain are not written in ink. They wait. What the Iron Captain does next was never recorded, because it has not happened yet — it happens when you decide it.

Reader: the story knows you are here. When you turn this page you will not be reading it any longer. You will be in it.
