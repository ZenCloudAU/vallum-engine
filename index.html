How it works

Choose an action

Each choice tests a different quality — not skill, character. Force breaks through. Restraint holds back. Witness reads clearly. Reputation commands.

Roll d20 + moral state

The dice show the result. Your moral state shapes the odds. High Force adds to force rolls. Low Restraint subtracts. Who you are changes what you can do.

The Hollow grows

Some choices cost Kael something he cannot name. Hollow tracks that cost. At seven it begins to change him. At ten the account is closed.
